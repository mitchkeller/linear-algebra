Theorem 4.27.

The determinants of elementary matrices have the following values:

  1. If \(E_1\) multiplies a row by a scalar \(\alpha\text{,}\) then \(\det(E_1) = \alpha\text{.}\)

  2. If \(E_2\) adds \(\alpha\) times a row to another row, then \(\det(E_2) = 1\text{.}\)

  3. If \(E_3\) swaps two rows, then \(\det(E_3) = -1\text{.}\)
